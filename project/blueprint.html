Site Plan – Gregorio Engenharia

Website Planning & Blueprint Document

Site Name

Gregorio Engenharia

The site name was selected to represent a professional engineering service focused on technical responsibility, credibility, and clarity. Using the family name reinforces trust and accountability, which are essential values in engineering and real estate regularization services.

Optional domain: gregorioengenharia.com.br

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide clear and accessible information about engineering services related to real estate regularization in Brazil. The site offers explanations of services such as Reurb, Usucapião, Habite-se, and construction regularization, as well as a consultation scheduling form and basic engineering calculation tools.

Scenarios

  • How can I regularize a property that was built without formal approval?
  • What documents and services do I need to legalize my property and how much will it cost?

Color Scheme

The following colors will be used consistently across the website:

Dark Blue
Medium Blue
  • Dark Blue: Headers, footer, navigation, emphasis areas.
  • Medium Blue: Buttons, links, highlights, call-to-action sections.
  • Light Gray: Page background.
  • White: Content cards and reading sections.

Typography

  • Primary Font: Arial / Helvetica – Used for all body text and forms due to its clarity and accessibility.
  • Headings: Same font with increased size and weight for consistency and professional appearance.
